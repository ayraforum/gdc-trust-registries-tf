# Trust Registries Track 1 — Policy, Taxonomy & Scope. Onboarding & Governance

The current v0.1 draft focuses on issuer trust registries: how issuer authority is represented, scoped, discovered, and relied upon without confusing registry participation with the source of that authority.

## Reading Path

1. [Start Here: Issuer Trust Registries](start-here-issuer-trust-registries.md) — a two-page plain-language introduction with a worked example, and the short set of decisions still to make together.
2. [SCOPE-Track1.md](SCOPE-Track1.md) — the track's defining document: what is in and out of scope, the repeating trust pattern, and the work items.
3. [Issuer Onboarding & Governance Framework v0.1](issuer-onboarding-and-governance-v0.1.md) — the guidance: shared vocabulary, authority options, the core model, the sovereignty boundary, and registry governance.

Deferred topics (verifier, relying-party, and wallet governance questions, data-model notes) are parked in the co-leads' [working repository](https://github.com/ayraforum/gdc-trtf-onboarding-and-governance/blob/main/later-phases/README.md).
